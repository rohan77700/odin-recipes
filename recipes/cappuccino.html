Cappuccino

Description

A cappuccino is an Italian coffee drink that is traditionally prepared with equal parts double espresso, steamed milk, and steamed milk foam on top. Cream may be used instead of milk.

Ingredients

  • 2 cups milk
  • 1 cinnamon stick
  • 2 cups strong coffee
  • Sugar (to taste)
  • Ground cinnamon

Preparation

  1. Step 1:

    2. In a saucepan heat the milk and the cinnamon stick just until milk comes to a boil.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Turn heat down and let milk simmer for 10 minutes.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Remove the cinnamon stick from the heated milk.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Pour 1/2 cup of coffee in the cup and add sugar to taste.

  5. Step 5:

    6. Now pour the hot milk, raising it high.

  6. Step 6:

    7. Sprinkle ground cinnamon and serve hot.
