Cheese Burst Pizza

Description

A cheese burst pizza is a pizza that has a generous amount of mozzarella cheese in the pizza's crust and base. Standard options only have it on top of toppings.

Ingredients

    for pizza dough:

  • 1/2 cup water (warm)
  • 1 tsp sugar
  • 1 tsp dry yeast
  • 2 cup maida / plain flour / refined flour
  • 1 tsp + 1 tsp olive oil
  • 1/2 tsp salt

    • for toppings:

  • 27 slices processed cheese
  • 2 tbsp pizza sauce
  • 1/2 cup mozzarella cheese
  • 3 slice tomato
  • few chopped capsicum
  • few chopped onion
  • 7 pieces jalapeno
  • 10 pieces olives
  • 1/4 tsp mixed herbs
  • 1/4 tsp chilli flakes

Preparation

    pizza dough preparation:

  1. Step 1:

    2. Firstly, in a large mixing bowl mix 1/2 cup warm water, 1 tsp sugar and 1 tsp dry yeast.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Rest and allow the yeast to activate.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Now add 2 cup maida, 1 tsp olive oil and 1/2 tsp salt.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Knead to smooth and soft dough adding more water if required.

  5. Step 5:

    6. Tuck the dough and form to a ball. place in a large mixing bowl.

  6. Step 6:

    7. Also grease the bowl from sides with olive oil to prevent dough from sticking to bowl.

  7. Step 7:

    8. Now cover the bowl tightly with cling wrap or plastic wrap and rest for about 2 hours.

  8. Step 8:

    9. After 2 hours, dough has raised and doubled in size indicating well fermented.


    cheese burst pizza preparation:

  1. Step 1:

    2. Punch the dough with fist to release down the air and knead slightly.

  2. Step 2:

    3. Divide the dough into 2 balls â€“ 1 large and another small one.

  3. Step 3:

    4. Take a smaller ball and dust with maida.

  4. Step 4:

    5. Roll slightly thick and make sure the size is smaller than the pizza base.

  5. Step 5:

    6. Then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork to prevent from fluffing up like chapati. keep aside.

  6. Step 6:

    7. Further place the larger dough ball on dusted pizza plate or tray. alternatively use cornmeal for more crunchy texture.

  7. Step 7:

    8. Flatten the dough by stretching with both hands.

  8. Step 8:

    9. Further, leaving a cm or more and create a dent. this helps the crust to fluff slightly.

  9. Step 9:

    10. Then prick with centre of dough with the help of fork to prevent pizza base from fluffing up like chapati.

  10. Step 10:

    11. Place the processed cheese slices inside leaving the sides. keep aside.

  11. Step 11:

    12. Take the rolled dough and roast on hot tawa.

  12. Step 12:

    13. Half cook both sides making sure not to cook completely.

  13. Step 13:

    14. Place the half cooked rolled dough over cheese placed pizza base.

  14. Step 14:

    15. Get the sides closer and seal tight. making sure there is no gap for the cheese to ooze out.

  15. Step 15:

    16. Furthermore, spread pizza sauce generously leaving the sides slightly.

  16. Step 16:

    17. Then spread 1/2 cup of grated mozzarella cheese over the base.

  17. Step 17:

    18. Also top with tomato slices, chopped capsicum, onions, jalapeno and olives.

  18. Step 18:

    19. And sprinkle 1/4 tsp chilli flakes, 1/4 tsp mixed herbs.

  19. Step 19:

    20. Further, bake in a preheated oven at 500 degrees fahrenheit or 200 to 250 degree celsius for about 15-20 minutes.

  20. Step 20:

    21. Finally, slice the cheese burst pizza and top with chilli flakes and oregano.
