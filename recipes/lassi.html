Mango Lassi

Description

Mango lassi is a delicious creamy drink with mango, yogurt, milk, a little sugar, and a sprinkling of cardamom. Itâ€™s cool and refreshing on a hot day!

Ingredients

  • 1 cup chopped very ripe mango (see how to peel and chop mango), frozen chopped mango, or canned mango pulp
  • 1 cup plain yogurt
  • 1/2 cup milk
  • 4 teaspoons honey or sugar, more or less to taste
  • Dash ground cardamom (optional)
  • Ice (optional)

Preparation

  1. Add ingredients to the blender:

    2. Put the mango, yogurt, milk, honey (or sugar), and cardamom into a blender and blend for 2 minutes.
    If you want a more milkshake-like consistency and it's a hot day, either blend in some ice as well or serve over ice cubes.

  2. Serve with a sprinkle of cardamom:

    3. Pour contents into a glass and sprinkle with a tiny pinch of ground cardamom to serve.
    The lassi can be kept refrigerated for up to 24 hours.
